Format of Game

  1. Eligible Participants can participate in the Game, which is a multiple choice question trivia game, which will be conducted during the Program.
  2. During the broadcast of the Program, the questions will be verbally asked by the anchor, to answer the questions, a unique identification number that is the “Jitthu” code (“Code”) will be displayed on the screen of the television for the corresponding question.
  3. In order to participate in the Game, You must enter the Code for every Question in the relevant field of the App and submit it.
  4. Upon submitting the Code on the App, the App will display 4 (four) answers in the English and in Tamil languages.
  5. You must then select 1 (one) answer from amongst the 4 (four) answers displayed on the App, within 20 (twenty) seconds or any other time period as intimated by STNL, of the answers being displayed on the App.
  6. If You do not submit an answer within the aforementioned time period, then You will be eliminated from the relevant Game. However, this will not impact your ability to participate in the Game on subsequent days.
  7. Upon submission of an answer on the App, in case the answer to the Question is:
  1. Incorrect, then You will be eliminated from the Game; or
  2. Correct, then You will be awarded a fixed number of points and will be allowed to answer the subsequent Questions.
  1. In order to be eligible to win the Game, You must select and submit the correct answers to all 10 (ten) Questions asked during the broadcast of the Program.

Winner

  1. The first 5 Participants who have accumulated the highest score by correctly answering all 10 (ten) Questions, in the least amount of time, will be considered as the winners of the Game for that relevant day (“Winners”).
  2. You agree that if You are declared as a Winner, then You will not be eligible to be declared as a Winner for a duration of 1 (one) month from the date of such relevant win. STNL may, in its sole discretion, award prize money to certain individuals from amongst the pool of Winners.
